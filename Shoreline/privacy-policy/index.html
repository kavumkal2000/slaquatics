Privacy Policy

Your Information, Handled Clearly

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Shoreline Aquatics values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through our website, booking requests, text messages, phone calls, social media, and other customer interactions, and how we use that information to operate our rental business.

Information we collect

We may collect information you choose to provide, including:

  • Name, phone number, email address, and preferred contact method
  • Requested rental dates, times, meeting spots, package details, and special instructions
  • Messages you send through phone, text, email, or web forms
  • Basic website usage information such as page visits and referring links

How we use your information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and confirm bookings
  • Coordinate delivery, scheduling, safety briefings, and customer support
  • Send updates about your request, reservation, payment, or waiver needs
  • Improve our website, services, pricing communication, and customer experience

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers who help us operate our business, such as payment processors, website platforms, email tools, or analytics providers, but only as needed for business operations.

Text messages and calls

If you contact Shoreline Aquatics by text message or provide your phone number during a booking request, we may use that number to communicate with you about your reservation, schedule changes, reminders, or support questions. Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier.

Cookies and analytics

Our site may use cookies, pixels, or analytics tools to understand traffic and improve performance. These tools may collect device, browser, and page interaction data. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed 100 percent secure. Please avoid sending sensitive payment or identity details through unsecured channels unless specifically requested through a trusted system.

Your choices

You can contact us to update or correct your information, ask us to stop contacting you for non-essential follow-up, or ask questions about how your data is being used.
