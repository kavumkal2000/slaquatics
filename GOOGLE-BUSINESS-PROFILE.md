# Google Business Profile (GBP) checklist — Shoreline Aquatics

For a local rental business, your **Google Business Profile drives more bookings than the website** — it's what shows up in the Map Pack and "jet ski rental near me" searches. Work through this once, then keep the recurring items going.

## One-time setup
- [ ] **Claim & verify** the profile at [business.google.com](https://business.google.com) (Shoreline Aquatics, Point Vista Rd, Hickory Creek, TX 75065, United States).
- [ ] **Primary category:** `Boat rental service` or `Jet ski rental service` (pick the closest). Add secondary categories: `Boat tour agency`, `Marina` if relevant.
- [ ] **NAP must match the website exactly:** Name "Shoreline Aquatics", Address "Point Vista Rd, Hickory Creek, TX 75065, United States", Phone "(469) 693-7164". Inconsistent NAP hurts ranking.
- [ ] **Hours:** Mon–Sun 8:30 AM – 8:30 PM (match the site). Set holiday hours when needed.
- [ ] **Website link:** `https://slaquatics.com` — and add the **booking link** `https://slaquatics.com/jetski-booking/` as the "Appointment/Bookings" URL.
- [ ] **Service area:** add Denton, Lewisville, Frisco, Flower Mound, The Colony, Little Elm, Highland Village (matches the landing pages).
- [ ] **Services:** add "Jet ski rental", "Pontoon boat rental", "Captained boat tour", "Aerial drone video" with short descriptions + prices ($56/hr jet ski, $120/hr boat, $50 drone).
- [ ] **Business description** (750 chars): lead with "Jet ski & pontoon boat rentals on Lake Lewisville in Hickory Creek, TX. Life jackets, fuel, and a full safety orientation included — no boating license required. Serving Denton, Lewisville, Frisco & the DFW area."
- [ ] **Attributes:** "Identifies as…", "Online appointments", "On-site services", wheelchair/parking if applicable.

## Photos (huge for rentals — profiles with photos get far more clicks)
- [ ] Logo + cover photo.
- [ ] 10–20 real photos: jet skis at the launch, the pontoon, happy customers (with permission), the lake, the drone shots.
- [ ] Add a few **short videos** (under 30s) — Google and customers both favor them.
- [ ] Keep adding new photos monthly (signals an active business).

## Reviews (the #1 ranking + conversion lever)
- [ ] Get your **review short-link** from the profile ("Ask for reviews" → copy link).
- [ ] Put that link in the ops tool's review automation (the Reviews page) and text it after every rental.
- [ ] **Reply to every review** within a day or two — thank the good ones, address the rare bad one calmly. Replies are a ranking signal.
- [ ] Aim for steady **review velocity** (a few new reviews every week beats a big burst).

## Recurring (monthly / ongoing)
- [ ] Post a **Google Post** weekly in season: availability, weekend deals, drone video clips, "book now" with the booking link.
- [ ] Answer **Q&A** on the profile (seed it yourself with the FAQs: license, cancellation, first-timers, what's included).
- [ ] Update **hours/season** (off-season closures, peak-season extended hours).
- [ ] Check **Insights** monthly: searches, calls, direction requests, website clicks — see what's working.

## Quick wins this week
1. Verify the profile + fix NAP to match the site exactly.
2. Upload 15 good photos.
3. Set the booking link as the Appointments URL.
4. Seed 5 Q&A entries and start texting the review link after rentals.
